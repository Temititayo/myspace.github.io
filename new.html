Me

Welcome to my space! I'm a Peace and Conflict Studies' practitioner with interests in data, technology, and traditional conflict resolution. I am also a chartered mediator, security and development practitioner, and a student of climate impact.

I explore how computational social science approaches can transform policy and fieldwork in the Global South.

This is a computational social scientist forecasting conflicts with NLP and networks!
