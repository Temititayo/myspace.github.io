About Me

Hello and welcome! I'm a lecturer in Peace and Conflict Studies with interests in data, technology, and traditional conflict resolution. I am also a chartered mediator, security and development practitioner, and a student of climate impact.

I explore how computational social science and African peacebuilding approaches can transform policy and fieldwork in the Global South.
